Protein Shakes

Peanut Butter Banana Protein Shake

Creamy, high-protein fuel for workouts or breakfast!

Ingredients:

  • 1 large ripe banana (frozen for thickness)
  • 2 tbsp natural peanut butter (or almond butter)
  • 1 scoop (30g) vanilla or chocolate whey protein
  • 1 cup (240ml) unsweetened almond milk (or dairy milk)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt (optional, for extra protein)
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • Ice cubes (as needed for thickness)

Instructions: Blend until smooth!

Fruit Shakes

Tropical Mango Pineapple Shake

A vitamin C-packed, hydrating delight!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (150g) frozen mango chunks
  • 1/2 cup (75g) frozen pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) coconut water (or orange juice)
  • 1/2 cup (120g) Greek yogurt (or dairy-free yogurt)
  • 1 tsp lime juice (for zing)
  • Ice cubes (optional, if fruit isn’t frozen)

Instructions: Blend until smooth and creamy!

Dessert Shakes

Salted Caramel Pretzel Shake

Sweet, salty, and ultra-indulgent.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (about 4 scoops) vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) caramel sauce (plus extra for drizzling)
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) whole milk (or heavy cream for extra richness)
  • 1/4 cup crushed pretzels (plus extra for topping)
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt (adjust to taste)
  • Whipped cream (for garnish)

Instructions: Blend until thick, then top with pretzel crumbs!
